Engineering Senior Design Project

Project Details

There is a bottleneck at Grocery Store checkouts. Self-Checkouts also experience long lines. Our Solution: An internet-connected shopping cart that lets you scan-as-you-shop. The Result: Parallelize the process while enhancing the experience for both the shopper and management.

This project took place over 3 quarters (minimum of 1200 work-hours). We got to take an idea from concept to production, and work as an interdisciplinary team in our own lab. This project allowed us to apply and exercise everything we've learned in our curriculum and ultimately prove ourselves as engineers, while also simulating a production in industry.
